✅ SafeTrack – Privacy Policy 

Privacy Policy for SafeTrack
Last Updated: 25/11/2025

SafeTrack (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of parents and children who use our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the SafeTrack child monitoring app and its related services.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Device & Usage Data (Child Device)

With parental authorization, SafeTrack may collect:

Live screen content

Camera stream (front/back)

Microphone audio triggered by parent

Real-time GPS location

Installed apps list & usage patterns

Device information (model, OS version, storage, battery)

Network information (WiFi/mobile network)

1.2 Parent Account Information

Phone number or email (optional based on your configuration)

Device ID

Login credentials (securely encrypted)

1.3 SOS & Alerts Data

Location

Emergency activity logs

1.4 Permissions

SafeTrack requires sensitive permissions such as Accessibility Service, Device Admin, Camera, Microphone, and Location to function. These permissions are used strictly for safety monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data only for these purposes:

To provide real-time monitoring for parents

To enable location tracking

To allow screen, camera, and mic monitoring

To send SOS alerts

To manage app restrictions

To improve app performance & security

We do not sell or rent any user data.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted (in transit and at rest).

We follow industry-standard security protocols.

Access to monitoring features is restricted to the parent account only.

No data is shared with third parties without consent.

4. Data Retention

Real-time streams (screen/mic/camera) are not stored unless the parent explicitly records them.

Logs and settings may be retained for operational purposes until a parent deletes their account.

5. Parental Consent

SafeTrack is designed only for parents to monitor their own children.
By installing the app on a child’s device, the parent confirms that:

They are the legal guardian,

They have full authorization to monitor the device.

6. Children’s Privacy

SafeTrack does not directly collect data from children without parental setup.
The app is not intended for independent use by children.

7. Data Sharing

We never share data except:

When required by law

When requested by the parent

When using secure server providers (e.g., Firebase/Supabase)

No data is used for advertising or analytics without consent.

8. User Rights

Parents may request:

Data deletion

Data export

Account removal
